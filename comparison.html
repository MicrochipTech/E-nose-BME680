Electronic Nose: MQ Array vs BME680

Two approaches to on-device odor classification on Adafruit Feather M4

🧪

  • Sensors1x Bosch BME680

  • ML Platformscikit-learn (local)
  • ModelC if/else (no runtime)
  • Flash Usage< 1 KB

  • Cost~$10

Key Innovation: Turbulence Relabeling

The BME680 approach eliminates the "turbulence" class entirely. Transition periods between scent jars are split at the slope reversal point — the first half keeps the fading scent's label, the second half gets the arriving scent's label. This means the model classifies correctly during transitions, not just after settling. Detection within ~10 seconds of a scent change.
